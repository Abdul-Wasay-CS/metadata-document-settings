Viewport & Responsive Design

This layout uses a simple, mobile-friendly design. Because the viewport is configured correctly, the page fits nicely on phones and tablets without forcing the user to zoom in.

Try removing the viewport meta tag from the <head> and reloading on a small screen. The browser will likely treat the page as much wider, shrinking everything to fit and making text hard to read.

Note: On desktop browsers, you may not see a difference when resizing the window.
