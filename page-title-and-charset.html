Title & Charset Example

This page uses <meta charset="UTF-8"> so we can safely display characters like:

  • Accented letters: café, naïve, façade
  • Non-Latin scripts: Привет, こんにちは, مرحبا
  • Emoji: 😊 🚀 🌍

If the encoding were wrong or missing, some of these characters might appear as question marks or boxes instead.
